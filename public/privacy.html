Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

SwissStartup Connect respects your privacy. This document outlines how we collect, store, and use data when you browse opportunities, create a profile, or collaborate with startups through our platform.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account details you provide such as name, email, and profile type.
  • Optional information about your skills, interests, or startup focus areas.
  • Usage details generated when you search, save, or apply to opportunities.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information only to recommend relevant roles, connect you with aligned teams, and improve the Swiss startup talent ecosystem. We never sell your personal data.

3. Your Rights

You can request a copy of your data, ask for corrections, or delete your profile at any time by emailing hello@swissstartupconnect.ch.

4. Contact

For privacy questions, contact our data protection lead at privacy@swissstartupconnect.ch.
